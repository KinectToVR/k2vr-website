Auto-start with SteamVR

You can make K2EX start automatically alongside SteamVR and also make it spawn trackers automatically when it is launched.


Enabling auto-start

You can go to SteamVR's settings window then in the Startup/Shutdown tab, pick the "Manage Overlays" option and turn on KinectToVR.

Next time you start SteamVR, K2EX will launch once VR is fully started (When you are in VR with the concentric circles on the ground)


Spawning trackers at launch

Alongside autostarting with SteamVR, you can have K2EX automatically spawn your trackers when it's launched. When enabled, the trackers will spawn 7 seconds after the app has started, and will only spawn if the SteamVR driver is working.
