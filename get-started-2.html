Compatible devices

Xbox 360 Kinect / Kinect for Windows V1

This is the recommended sensor. It supports all features, such as foot rotation and turning around.


Xbox One Kinect / Kinect for Windows V2

This works with KinectToVR but it has many hardware and software issues that may hamper your experience.

We strongly recommend AGAINST buying the Xbox One Kinect for reasons stated on --> this page

PlayStation Move + PlayStation Eye

Requires 3 PS Move controllers and at least 2 PS Eye cameras. The hardware and system resource requirements are much higher. While the tracking quality is excellent (equivalent to Vive trackers) setting it up is not for the faint of heart.
