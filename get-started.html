Before we begin

System requirements

  • Cloud PCs (Shadow, Plutosphere) are not compatible!
  • Virtual machines work if a USB host controller is passed through
  • Windows 10 1809 or newer
  • SteamVR-compatible headset
  • Xbox 360 Kinect, Xbox One Kinect or Playstation Move + Eye
  • USB adapter for Kinect
  • Compatible USB 3 controller for Xbox One Kinect
Read more about system requirements

You need a roomscale VR headset with tracked motion controllers

This app is only for lower body (waist and feet) tracking. It supports all headsets that work with SteamVR and have tracked controllers.
You must have roomscale tracking for your head and hands. (Being able to freely move around your room.)


You need a large enough room

When using Kinect, you should stand at least 6 feet (2 meters) away from the sensor and have it at head height pointing down at you.
You should be standing 9 feet (3 meters) away for the best tracking.
